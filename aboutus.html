About Us


DELISH House website, built specifically for the lonely people who were getting bored by their daily life centers which is dedicated to teaching and involve learners in cooking. DELISH House is the region’s leading food-focused website that celebrates the unique Asian culinary experience.
As of 6 March 2018, Delish House (part of Scripps Networks Interactive) has been acquired and is now part of Discovery, Inc.
We relaunched the website in August 2018 with a vision to help you get inspiration and recipes to create quick, simple and delicious Asian-inspired home-cooked meals for you and your family.
On November 29 2019, DELISH House became Asian Food Network, a brand new digital and linear experience re-imagining Asian Food Channel beyond television, with new features, a modern look and new brand promise.

Our goal:

“Be open to new ideas and approaches. Develop your cooking skills.”
